What is MVG Golf?

 

Minimum Viable Golf is a startup golf academy dedicated to improving pace of play.

 

MVG - The Swing

The Minimum Viable Golf Swing is the most efficient swing.

 

MVP - The Club

The Minimum Viable Golf Club is truly one club. Golf clubs are designed to either deliver a descending or an ascending blow to the golf ball.

 

MVP - The Game

The Minimum Viable Game of Golf is the set of rules participating golfers agree upon before the game begins
